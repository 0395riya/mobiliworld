About Us

Oberoitel Group has been a key player in the telecom sector since 1997, creating value in the retail space. The promoters of this organisation established it with the goal and objective of adding value for retail customers. The group started with Telecom products which included Mobile Phones and later added Tablets, attachment essentials like Smart Watches, Audio Accessories and others.

Mobiliti World

"Mobiliti World" Powered by Oberoitel, as the name suggests, is a leading brand that is in the core business of retailing Mobile devices and accessories in the Telecom Eco System. The company was set up in 2013 by a group of leaders and individuals who came together from other leading retail chains. This team brought great value to the table. With 350+ outlets across Delhi, NCR, and West Bengal, the company has carved out a niche for itself among India's preferred retail brands with a business of over Rs 250 crore per annum.

The team at Mobiliti World strives to maintain long-term relationships with the brands and customers. The company sources products directly from the brands or their approved channel partners. The company strives to provide the best possible service to its customers.
